Casey Stengel is credited with asking about the lovably awful 1962 Mets: "Can't anybody here play this game?" He might have been talking about the management of the 2019 Knicks. They have lots of cap space, the profits from giving up on Kristaps Porzingas, which was sad (and it turns out delusional), but it's also power, even if they didn't get KD or Kawhi -- and of course they're pissing it away. We need this team to be sold. Please anyone, my friend's cat even, could run this team better. #
